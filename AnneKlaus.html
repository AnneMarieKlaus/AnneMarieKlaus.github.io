  1. Learn how to live out of a friend's house as a family for a month.
  2. Live in an RV for almost 2 months while exploring San Diego to Vancouver.
  3. Open our hearts outgrageously to Synchronicity...what a bless-ed flow!
  4. Family Reunions, Collge Reunions, High School Reunions, and dear friend reunions.
  5. Get up close and personal with the powerful force of glaciers on a cruise to Alaska and a helicopter ride in the Canadian Rockies.
  6. Initiate a baby boy to join our family. ;)
  7. Live in Germany! for 3 months, learning the language along side my daughter.
  8. Live near Vienna, Austria for another month and a half, and spend our first Christmas with my German mother-in-law.
  9. Soul search, soul search, soul search.
  10. And then, once back in Charlotte, HOME search, HOME search, HOME search.
  11. Realize Coding and Programming fit right into this blessed life of ours.
